TOP - Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

Description

A wonderfully delicious mix of peanut butter, jelly or jam, and bread. A classic

Ingredients

  • 2X Slices of bread - Wonderbread recommended
  • Peanut Butter of your choice
  • Jelly or Jam of your choice
  • Butter Knife

Steps

  1. Using butter knife Spread peanut butter on one side of each piece of bread
  2. Place the bread so the bare sides are touching the plate
  3. Rinse/clean butter knife
  4. Spread jam or jelly of your choice on-top of the peanut butter on one or both slices of bread
  5. Place bread so that sides with spread are touching
  6. Serve the sandwhich
