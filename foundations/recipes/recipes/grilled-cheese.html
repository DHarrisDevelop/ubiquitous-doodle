TOP - Grilled Cheese Sandwhich

Description

A wonderfully delicious mix of butter (or other oil), carbs (bread), and fantastic melty cheese. The ULTIMATE comfort food

Ingredients

  • 2X Slices of bread - Wonderbread recommended
  • 1 OR 2 Slices of Chesse - American melts best
  • (Softened) Butter - Whipped or spreadable butter works best
  • Heat Source - Stove, Range, Campfire
  • Frypan
  • Spatula
  • Butter Knife

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of each piece of bread
  2. Place the buttered bread so the buttered sides are touching and the unbuttered side is showing on the top bread
  3. Place cheese slice(s) on the top bread
  4. Preheat the frypan using the heat source to medium high heat
  5. Once preheated, place the top slice of bread with the cheese on the fry pan buttered side down
  6. Using spatula periodically move the bread around the frypan to ensure the bread doesn't burn
  7. Once the cheese starts getting melty, place the second bread on-top of the cheese with the unbuttered side touching the cheese and the buttered side up
  8. Using the spatula, flip the sandwhich over so that you are now cooking the uncooked piece of bread
  9. Using the spatula continue to move the sandwhich so that the bread doesn't get burned
  10. Once both sides are grilled to your liking, serve the sandwhich - recommend cutting this in half
