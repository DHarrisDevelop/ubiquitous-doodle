TOP - Mac N Cheese

Description

A wonderfully delicious mix of butter, carbs (pasta elbows), and fantastic melty cheese sauce. A comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Macoroni Elbows
  • Cream Cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Heat Source - Stove, Range, Campfire
  • Pot
  • Cooking Spoon

Steps

  1. Boil Macaroni Elbows until al-dente
  2. Drain the Macaroni Elbows
  3. Place Butter in pan over low heat
  4. Place milk in pan over low heat
  5. Return macaroni noodles to pan
  6. Place Cream Cheese and shredded cheese in pan
  7. Over low heat, continue to stir all ingredients until desired consistency is reached
